## What was adapted

The general concept of modular, file-based skills for AI agents, and parts of
the structural approach (one `SKILL.md` per skill, example files) were
influenced by the `anthropics/skills` repository.

All skill *content* in this repository is original and written specifically for
this project.

---

## Modified for compatibility with

- [Codex CLI](https://github.com/openai/codex)
- Local AI agent frameworks

---

## How to cite upstream skills

If you add a skill that closely mirrors an upstream skill, include a line at
the bottom of the `SKILL.md`:

```markdown
## References

- Adapted from https://github.com/anthropics/skills (MIT License)
```
